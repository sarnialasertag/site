Welcome Adventurers!

Step into Sarnia's go-to spot for nonstop fun and chill vibes. Take aim in our multi-level laser tag arena—loaded with sneaky hideouts and perfect perch spots. Scale new heights on the rock climbing wall and put your skills to the test. Or slow things down with a round of mini golf, where each hole adds its own twist. Whether you're chasing thrills or just looking to unwind, this is your place to do it all.

Our Activities

From action-packed to casual, we've got something for everyone

Laser Tag

Our laser tag arena features a full two-storey layout designed for nonstop action. Navigate winding corridors, surprise your opponents from elevated spots, and make the most of every level. With intense, multi-level gameplay, every match feels like a real mission.

Rock Climbing

Take on the challenge of our rock climbing wall and see how far you can push yourself. Every grip and step tests your strength, focus, and determination. Whether you're racing to the top or climbing at your own pace, it's all about beating your own best and enjoying the climb.

Mini Golf

Looking for something laid-back but still a good time? Jump into a round of mini golf. Our colourful course is full of clever obstacles and sneaky turns that keep things interesting. Line up your shot, go for that hole-in-one, and enjoy some easygoing competition along the way.

Group Booking FAQs

How does group pricing work?

It's simple!

Our group packages are priced per person and include access to all listed activities within that package, as well as a private party room. To qualify for group pricing and the private room, each group must consist of at least 6 participants. Please note, the maximum number of people per group is limited to 20 at any time.

When you arrive for your event, we will check you in, confirm the number of guests and finalize payment. We do not take payments online.

Are you looking to book for a larger event (20+ people) for a business, school or organization? Reach out to us directly.

Is there a minimum or maximum amount of people?

Yes!

To qualify for group pricing and the private room, each group must consist of at least 6 participants. Please note, the maximum number of people per group is limited to 20 at any time.

Will my group have a private room available to them?

Yes!

Throughout your booking slot, you'll have exclusive access to a private party room. It's your space to decorate and use as needed for your event. Enjoy the privacy and flexibility to make your celebration perfect!

Can we bring outside food or drinks?

Yes!

You're welcome to bring your favorite treats, like a birthday cake or snacks, along with non-alcoholic beverages to enjoy during your event. Additionally, we offer a selection of personal-sized snacks including chips and chocolate bars, as well as soda and water available for purchase, should you need a little extra refreshment.

Please note that The Arena maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy regarding the presence and use of alcohol or drugs on the premises.

We are committed to providing a safe and enjoyable environment for all guests. If any member of your party is found violating this policy, the entire group will be required to leave immediately, and no compensation will be offered for the disruption. We appreciate your cooperation and understanding in helping us maintain a safe and welcoming space for everyone.

We encourage all guests to read our Respect & Responsible Use Policy before visiting or booking to ensure you're familiar with all our venue guidelines.
